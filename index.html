About Me

Elsa brings a unique and worldly perspective to her current position as an agent. Having transferred to several different countries throughout her childhood education, Elsa’s adaptive nature was able to come to fruition, a quality that serves her well as a real estate agent. Elsa can also speak fluent Chinese(中文), fluent Korean(한국말), and English, making her the perfect candidate to communicate with clients who come from multiethnic backgrounds.

A post-graduate from Middlesex University, London with a MSc in Finance and Investment, Elsa possesses a keen sense for commercial real estate market and return on the investment for local and international investors. She understands the importance of providing a truly personalized service to her clients and ensures she delivers the utmost attention every client deserves.
